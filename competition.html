RoboClash 2025: Rescue Rally

Competition Details

Date: 8 - 10 May 2025

Time: 08:30 - 19:00

Location: Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)

Team Composition: 2-6 participants

Registration fee: $50 (non-refundable)

Hardware: Cocorobo kits

Software: CocoBlockly

Theme Description:
In recent years, the intensification of climate change has resulted in increasingly frequent and destructive natural disasters. These supernatural events pose a significant threat to the stability of our natural and man-made systems.

Situated in a flood-struck city, RoboClash: Rescue Rally will see teams race against the clock to clear debris and evacuate citizens. Beware of the terrain's lingering and tough challenges!
