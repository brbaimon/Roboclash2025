About RoboClash

RoboClash is an exciting robotics competition designed to challenge creativity, technical skill, and innovation among participants that has been held in SUTD since 2019. Teams compete to design, build, and program robots capable of completing specific tasks or overcoming obstacles in a dynamic, competitive environment.

The event fosters a spirit of collaboration and ingenuity, bringing together students, professionals, and robotics enthusiasts to showcase cutting-edge technology and problem-solving skills. With challenges ranging from autonomous navigation to head-to-head robot battles, RoboClash offers a thrilling platform to inspire innovation and celebrate the future of robotics.
